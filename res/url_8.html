Manual Scavenging in India

'Low castes still forced to clean human excreta'

Hundreds of thousands of impoverished "low caste" Indians are being forced to clean human excreta from dry toilets and open drains, despite a ban on the discriminatory and undignified practice, Human Rights Watch has said. India has since Independence enacted laws aimed at ending the practice of manual scavenging but centuries-old feudal, caste-based customs have allowed it to persist.  Full Article

    Why inflation is so persistent

    By D H Pai Panandiker

    Inflation has been high for nearly four years and has not responded to RBI or government policies. This is because the kind of inflation that we have is of an unusual variety and cannot be checked by conventional means. Two types of food are critically important. If production of vegetables and milk increases to catch up with demand, food inflation will drop by two percentage points.  Full Article
