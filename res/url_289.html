If Mindy Kaling Guest Stars On 'Scandal,' She Plans To Have A Sex Tape With Idris Elba

The Huffington Post   |  By Leigh Weingus
Posted: 09/12/2014 3:19 pm EDT Updated: 3 hours ago
  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Email
  • Comment
  • tumblr
  • stumble
  • reddit

It's no secret that Shonda Rhimes will appear on "The Mindy Project" this fall, and it looks like Mindy Kaling has some big plans if the "Scandal" creator decides to return the favor.

Speaking with HuffPost Live's Ricky Camilleri on Friday, "The Mindy Project" creator and star opened up about her dream "Scandal" role.

"I would play myself, and I would hire Olivia Pope to get me out of some horrible, disgusting scandal," Kaling said.

"I'd let Shonda come up with it, but if she wanted me to do a sex tape with Idris Elba, I would do it," she continued. "That's the story now Shonda, so I guess that's what you have to do."

"The Mindy Project" returns Sept. 16 at 9:30 p.m. on Fox.
