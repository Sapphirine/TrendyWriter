About The Third Metric

The current model of success -- which equates success with burnout, sleep deprivation, and driving ourselves into the ground -- isn't working. Following the success of the inaugural Third Metric conference in June 2013, Arianna Huffington and Mika Brzezinski hosted a second Third Metic New York (April 24-25), focusing on redefining success to include well-being, wisdom, wonder, compassion and giving. Speakers included Katie Couric, Julianne Moore, Aetna CEO Mark Bertolini and many more. The conversation from the conference continues on The Huffington Post.
