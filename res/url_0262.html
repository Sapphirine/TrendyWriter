Viral Reality
Monrovia, Liberia—A worker sprays the body of an Ebola victim, just one of an estimated 413 people to die from the deadly virus in Liberia. The situation was exacerbated when locals mobbed an Ebola isolation ward in this capital city, reportedly upset that sick people were being brought there. “Members of this mob literally pulled people out of the isolation ward,” a photographer told NPR. “I saw a man carrying a small girl by one arm up in the air and she was screaming, and the crowd carried them off.” Doctors Without Borders is opening a new center near Monrovia that can hold hundreds of Ebola patients. The World Health Organization estimates that as many as 1,145 people have died in the current outbreak.
