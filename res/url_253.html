Desecrating Dr. Maya Angelou's Funeral

When news circulated that the notorious Westboro Baptist Church was planning to protest the "home-going" service of our nation's most beloved citizen, poet, author, civil-rights activist, and sister sage to us all, Dr. Maya Angelou, there was a collective gasp of disbelief.

Irene Monroe

Nate Phelps

Executive Director, Calgary Centre for Inquiry; member of the board of directors, Recovering from Religion; LGBT advocate

Interdisciplinary creative; words, pixels, pigments. Activist, advocate, adjudicator, social media integrator.

Brent Almond

Writer, graphic designer, illustrator and dad

Be Grateful to Homophobic Preacher

Why I Don't Grieve For Fred Phelps
