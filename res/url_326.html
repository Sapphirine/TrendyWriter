Looking for I Want Media?

Thanks to all those who have supported I WANT MEDIA over the years. We have currently put the I WANT MEDIA newsletter on hold and will continue to keep our subscribers updated on our plans here. Going forward, the Adweek team is focusing on the development of new offerings that incorporate the best of I WANT MEDIA and our current products.

Rest assured that subscribers data will not be sold or used for any other purposes. Users will not be be automatically subscribed to other newsletters and all I WANT MEDIA subscription data will be destroyed in a reasonable time period.

We thank you for your encouragement and patience. To continue receiving daily updates of media news, please consider subscribing to one of the other outstanding newsletters offered by Adweek and Mediabistro.
