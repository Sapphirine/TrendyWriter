About Ferguson

On Aug. 9, 2014, 18-year-old Michael Brown was shot to death by police officer Darren Wilson in Ferguson, a city located in St. Louis, Missouri. This event involving Brown, who was black and unarmed, and Wilson, who is white, immediately incited a series of protests fueling racial tensions in the community. It also prompted stories, reports and opinions from people and events around the nation, many of which are gathered here to reflect on the incidents that happened in Ferguson on Aug. 9, and all days following.
