Strike Gold With This Cool Earring Set

In the wise words of Jennifer Lopez, don't be fooled by these rocks. This stud earring set resembles tiny geometric nuggets of gold, but the price won't make you broke, and you can choose from three different sizes. Wear the smaller ones on a casual work Friday, and reserve the bolder ones to add glint to your simple jean and tee weekend look.
