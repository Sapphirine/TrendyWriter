Mika Brzezinski: 'The NFL Supports Domestic Violence'

The Huffington Post  | By Jack Mirkinson
Posted: 09/09/2014 8:23 am EDT Updated: 09/09/2014 8:59 am EDT

Mika Brzezinski had this to say about the NFL's handling of the Ray Rice scandal:

“It is a culture that clearly puts up with domestic violence. It is a culture that puts up with and accepts and even supports domestic violence...that woman is a victim...the NFL supports domestic violence by not even, I mean it doesn’t even seem like he’s kicked out! Suspended indefinitely? Are they hoping things are gonna get better? No! That is a deal breaker. It should be over. There are things you accept in relationships with people. There are things you accept in partnerships, and on teams. There are things you don’t accept.”
