On what would've been Paul Walker's 41st birthday, his daughter Meadow took to social media to honor the special occasion.

The teenager posted a touching throwback photo of her with her father on her Instagram account Sept. 12, writing, "Happy birthday, I love you":

Meadow Walker is the only child of the "Fast & Furious" star, who died in November 2013.

The late actor's "Fast & Furious" co-stars also paid tribute to him via social media. Ludacris shared a photo of Walker helping a young child, writing, "Happy Bday to an Angel today @paulwalker":

Walker founded Reach Out WorldWide, a non-profit disaster relief organization, in 2010.
